About Us:

Welcome to Dead Moon Games, your ultimate destination for a variety of exciting games! Our website offers popular titles such as Slope, Monkey Mart, 1v1.lol, Duck Life, Five Nights at Freddy's, Run 3, Subway Surfers, and many more. Our games are curated with the intention of being accessible on school Chromebooks, allowing students to play during free time, lunch breaks, or whenever they feel like having some fun (with permission, of course).

Please note that while we aim to provide an enjoyable gaming experience, we are not responsible for any off-task behavior that may occur. Dead Moon Games was created with the intention of offering a platform for students to unwind and have fun responsibly.
